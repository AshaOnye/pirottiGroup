Arc. Abdullahi Suleiman
Chairman/CEO

Kasha M. Iliya brings a decade of multidisciplinary expertise across Business Strategy, International Public Relations, Trade and Investment, and Development Consulting within the realms of both public and private sectors.

With a Master of Science (MSc.) degree in Architecture from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Abdullahi brings forth over 12 years of profound expertise in architectural design, construction, regulatory compliance, and project management. His diverse background spans both public and private sectors, honing his skills across various facets of the industry.
A distinguished member of the Nigerian Institute of Architects and licensed by the Architects Registration Council of Nigeria (ARCON), Abdullahi's influence extends beyond Pirotti Projects Ltd. He serves as a Non-Executive Director on the board of NGX Real Estate Limited (NGX RelCo), contributing invaluable insights and shaping the industry landscape.

His tenure at the Federal Ministry of Housing and Urban Development fortified his comprehensive understanding of the housing sector. Beyond his professional endeavors, Abdullahi's fervor for exploring diverse cultures through travel and his affinity for nature complement his holistic approach to life. At the helm of Pirotti Group Ltd., Abdullahi orchestrates a vision that intertwines creativity, sustainability, and pioneering leadership. His commitment to excellence and transformative solutions is the guiding force propelling the company toward new heights of innovation and success.
